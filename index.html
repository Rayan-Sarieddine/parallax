Welcome!

Once upon a time on a sunny beautiful day, there was Labib, sitting on a bench contemplating life.
Labib is very lost in life and is wondering what he could study in order to become a very successful man with a reputable profession.
Labib suddenly realizes that software engineers are very highly in demand now adays and it is a very respectable job that pays extremely well. He therefore decides to enroll in a 14 week full stack web development bootcamp.
After getting accepted, Labib jumps out of joy and tells himself that it is going to be a very easy and straightforward journey to complete the bootcamp.
Little did he know that he will be signing off his life and is then faced with the hard truth, that the journey is extremely difficult where sleep and fun time and chilling with the boys will be sacrificed. He even got asked to dump his girlfriend. But nevertheless, Labib is committed and wants to share his learning journey with us.
